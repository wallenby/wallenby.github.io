Custom Camera Project

I love photography and cameras! I've always been fascinated with the way old and new cameras worked. I read and learned about all their inner workings on my free team because I was so entriguied as to how they worked! I'm also a big sucker for making projects with the Raspberry Pi, so when I saw that the Pi foundation made a high quality camera module, I knew what my next big project was gonna be. I started off by putting the parts I needed together.

  • A computer to run everything (the Raspberry Pi 4)
  • sensor - The HQ Camera Module
  • A screen to see what's the sensor is seeing
  • A battery, otherwise I'd be limited to indoor usage.
  • Buttons to control the screen since bringing a keyboard around to type lines into the terminal wasn't my idea of practical
  • A case to hold everything.
